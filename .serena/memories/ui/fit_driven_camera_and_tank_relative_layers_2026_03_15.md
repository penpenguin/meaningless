2026-03-15: `src/components/aquariumLayout.ts` now owns fit-driven camera framing for `AdvancedScene`. `resolveDefaultCameraPosition()` and `resolvePhotoModeCameraPosition()` accept optional `(dimensions, fov, aspect)` and solve camera Z by matching projected front-glass width coverage instead of using fixed width-based Z ratios. Standard framing targets about 82% width coverage at 16:9; photo mode uses the same solver with slightly tighter coverage (~85.5%) plus the existing lateral/target bias. `AdvancedScene` now refreshes default/photo camera positions and targets from viewport aspect in `setupCamera()` and `handleResize()`. Backdrop / backdrop overlay / midground / foreground shadow anchors are centralized in `AQUARIUM_DEPTH_LAYER_ANCHORS` and resolved with `resolveTankRelativePosition()`, so Y/Z stay dimension-relative when tank size changes. Regression coverage lives in `src/components/AdvancedScene.test.ts` for 16:9, 4:3, ultrawide, and expanded tank dimensions.
