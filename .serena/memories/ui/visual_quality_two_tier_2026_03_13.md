2026-03-13: Visual quality settings were simplified from three tiers to two tiers. `QualityLevel` is now `'simple' | 'standard'`. Default quality for new saves/settings is `simple`. Legacy migration maps `low -> simple` and `medium/high -> standard` in both `src/game/gameSave.ts` and `src/utils/settingsSchema.ts`. `GameHudOverlay` now renders two Japanese labels (`簡易`, `標準`) and dispatches the same `SETTINGS/SET_QUALITY` action. `AdvancedAquariumScene` keeps shadows and texture layers active even on `simple`; the tier difference now primarily adjusts pixel ratio, shadow map type/size, hero lighting layers, and particle/fish density. Initial renderer antialiasing is chosen from the boot quality by passing the persisted quality from `AdvancedAquariumApp` into the `AdvancedAquariumScene` constructor.
