Introduced a static content registry under src/content/ to centralize fish and decor definitions. Fish are now split into one-module-per-species in src/content/fish/*.ts and decor into src/content/decor/*.ts. src/content/registry.ts builds the registry, validates duplicate IDs, and exposes getters/list/starter selectors. Existing src/game/catalog.ts and src/utils/speciesCatalog.ts were reduced to compatibility selectors backed by the registry. Runtime consumers now read content directly from the registry in gameReducer, gameSave, simulation, DetailedFish, GameHudOverlay, and profileSchema. Adding a new fish now means creating one fish module and exporting it from src/content/fish/index.ts.
